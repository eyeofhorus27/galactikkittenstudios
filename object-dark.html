I am Vasilia Niles

Design is my religion. Everything was designed for a role and aesthetic. I leave nothing to chance. If it's there, it's there for a reason. Even when there's no reason, that is the reason. I want anything and everything I create to inspire you to ask questions, to be immersive, to make a bold statement and to be an experience. If it's a User Interface I want it to be intuitive yet unique; purposeful yet exploratory. If it's physical, I want it to remove you from your reality and to make you reinvent yourself.

As an avid futurist and manic traveler, I am an early adopter who predicts global trends and possesses the empathy to generate solutions for people of all walks of life. The spectrum of our personal experience is that of one of the colors of the rainbow when in reality there are so many more. If everyone was blind would color still exist? This is how we need to approach the world.

For work inquires shoot me an e-mail.

Backstory

My background was originally in film directing. As a result I developed a quick eye for what works and what doesn't. Currently I'm an unconventional Creative Director and UX/UI Designer. I have designed and built sites & apps for all types of needs. For Hypnotherapists, queer performance groups, innovativ startups & even for festival/event companies.

In addition, I deal with anything that has to do with content creation, especially for marketing, from photography for travel blogs to copy for describing bizzare inventions. Finally, as both a big picture thinker and deconstructer, I'm great at consulting companies on how to devise their brand and marketing strategies by breaking them down into effective and actionable steps.
