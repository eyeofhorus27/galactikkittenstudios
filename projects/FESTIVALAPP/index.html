  • Festival App

    UX/UI Design

  • Client
    Entertainment Startup

  • Role
    UX/UI Designer

For this project I was asked to design a demo UI of the app you see above to present to potential investors. The app would contain a comprehensive list of all the major festivals around the world and would act as a type of personalized blog. Users can view all the different kinds of festivals all in one place, learn about them, save their favorite ones, write stories regarding their experiences and purchase tickets.

Festivals are known for being awe-inspiring so the app needed to be image heavy to create that excitement you get right before a festival. The butteflies in your stomach as you prep to embark on your journey. The user persona for this particulat target group happened to be something I understood very well since I'm part of the dmeographic. Therefore, I had to create something intuitive, addicting and clean but with a lot of visual stimuation. A bit like Instagram.

Regarding the app colors, I chose white for the background, gray for the header section and black or white text so that it remained neutral enough to accomodate the colorful imagery. For this one I experimented a lot with different user flows & journeys utilizing Flinto to build a prototype and test on users. I got to test it out on users while at festivals, so it gave me a lot of insight on how individuals use apps when in environments with lots of stimuli.
