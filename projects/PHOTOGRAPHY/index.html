  • Role
    Photographer & Creative Director

Photography was the child of film directing. Having to occasionally take on the role of DP (thanks to budget), I acquired my own DSLR and equipment. After shooting a bunch of people bathing in lasers at festivals and a few monkeys scratching their genitals in Bali, I was hired to shoot events and travel destinations all around the world. I have even made money on Shutterstock and it's more than a couple dollars. I love shooting and I am blessed to say it has taken me to many cool places I might have otherwise never been to. Because of my background in film directing and working in the art department for film & theatre, I oftentimes also get hired to art direct shoots whether that means executing someone else's vision or coming up with my own, depending on the client's needs. My specialties are usually travel, fashion shoot or very stylized concepts such as Vaporwave and Steampunk.
