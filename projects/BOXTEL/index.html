Boxtel is my own startup that I created with two other business partners. It is a modular pop up hotel made of sustainable and luxurous upcycled containers. As one of the co-founders my job was to create our marketing strategy, design the The Boxtel App in Sketch and build the website for bookings on Wordpress. For the marketing strategy I included things like user personas, competitive & SWOT analysis, social media strategy, timeline for execution and budget. Our guests are usually people between the ages of 24-35 that have flexible but high-paying jobs such as art directors, consultants and marketers. These individuals love adventure and value sustainability and nature so they are our ideal customer since most of our Boxtels are by the beach, the forest or the mountain.

For the app I designed some demo views of what it will include. We wanted the app to be all inclusive yet intuitive and simple. Based on branding guidelines I created 2 versions. One where the menu was comprised mainly of images and in the other version I had the Smarthome section made of simple flat icons of each room. Ultimately we went with the simple icons because we favored simplicity of use over visually stimulating views.

For the website, I wanted it to feel familiar to other hotel sites. The concept of staying in a container is so unique, I wanted to at least build a sense of trust for our users with something a bit more familiar. Aesthetically I used a lot of HDR images of the locations to make it feel like a surreal dream. Staying in a Boxtel is a one of a kind experience that will have you seeing the world in HD.
