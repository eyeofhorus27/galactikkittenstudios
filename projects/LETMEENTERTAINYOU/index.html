Alright this is where I get to show off. I'm also (and have been my whole life) a performer. So I craved for a website to showcase my work. However, this ended up turning more into a form of artwork/autobiography where I get to be my most authentic digital self! I added patterns & textures you would see at my intergalactic gypsy caravan.

I hang up frames with my photography and modeling shots on my digital walls, put my arts & crafts on a cyber-display and built a little corner to express my inner world aka a blog for my poetry. However, because the self-ingulgence wasn't enough, I needed to satisfy my inner ADHD child with fidget spinners and slides, hence all the wacky micro-interactions. In other words, I made the website my residence and playground. Take a look...
