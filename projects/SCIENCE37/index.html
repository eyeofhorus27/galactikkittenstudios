  • Science 37

    Interior Design Creative Direction

  • Role
    Web Designer, UX/UI Designer, Project & Account Manager

I joined Science37 mainly because of the company's mission. Their mission is to make clinical trials accessible for all and speed up the time it takes for medications to reach the market. Science37 achieves this is via conducting virtual clinical trials. I worked at Science37 for almost 3 years and I started off as a software engineer/UI designer but later transitioned to a tech/creative project and account manager role.

UX/UI:

• Worked closely with members of Clinical Operations for feedback on usability and user flow to better understand areas of concern and improve overall user experience. Purpose was to create a seamless and intuitive design for our app, NORA.
• Assembled a design and development team for the creation of a luxury Healthcare magazine, “Docere.”
• Led efforts with the lead UX designer to create a Design System for our main Product, NORA, based on Brandbook Guidelines.
• Assisted in the full redesign of our Marketing Site based on our new Design System.
• Utilized both Agile and Waterfall methodologies depending on project to identify early roadblocks, gather clear specs, set realistic deadlines and efficiently balance stakeholder needs with viable results.

Web Development:

• Built new features (both front-end and back-end), identified and eliminated bugs and developed new logic for generating reports based on HIPPA regulations. This resulted in improved quality of NORA, our company’s software and ensured more reliable data.
• Translated mockups into fully responsive pages.
• Taught Angular 2 classes to our team of developers to ensure a smooth transition of our app to this Framework.

Project/Account Manager:

• Oversaw and successfully carried out some of the company’s biggest commercial campaigns featuring multiple ads on CNN, Pandora, Facebook, World Cup, Telemundo and NBA Playoffs from concept creation to execution via constant communication with clients, the team & stakeholders, diligent prioritization, reevaluation of needs and iterative creative reviews while navigating heavy Healthcare regulations.
• Invented an effective workflow for communication between our Media & Marketing, Tech and Patient Affairs Depts from project requests to execution. This resulted in a faster development process and decreased budget spending.
• Coordinated and supervised projects via Mavenlink (for Marketing) and JIRA (for Tech – 2 week sprints) for better task delegation and timeline visualization.
• Regularly updated data storing methodologies, naming conventions and created Lucid Charts of Workflows & Processes.
