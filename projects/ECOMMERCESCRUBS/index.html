  • E-Commerce Site Redesign

    UX/UI Design

  • Client
    Scrubs Clothing Company

  • Role
    UX/UI Designer

For this project I was asked to redesign the flow where users view a specific product. The company felt the UI for this section was a bit confusing and therefore wanted to make the specs more clear as well as provide suggestions to upsell to their customers.

To make things more intuitive and easier for the user, I reorganized the IA of the specs from the most important to the least important. I made the text a bit larger and made the fonts, colors and styling more cohesive to build more trust. I added a cart icon to where it once just said cart since the mind understands better in symbols. Finally, I turned the product images into an interactive card that you flip over to get suggestions on what to match the item with.
