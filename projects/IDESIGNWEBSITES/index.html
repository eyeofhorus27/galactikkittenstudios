This is the first website I ever created right out of my coding bootcamp to showcase my work. I wanted it to be fun and quirky just like my personality. I wanted to attract the creative, "out of the box" type of clients who would hire me to build an online art gallery, an app for raves or even an interactive children's e-learning site. I ended up getting a job as a Software Engineer at a Biotech company instead but that's a story for another time.

I don't do backend development any longer (so for GOD'S SAKE please DO NOT contact me for a full-stack engineering job) but I'm showcasing it here to get an idea of my personal brand and style. Peachy pastels, digital textures you want to touch, interactive icons that spin and illustrations that open dominate the site.

I wanted the site to feel like a scrap book. Something you can touch and feel that's personal yet fun. By the time you're done exploring my site, even if my style doesn't resonate with you, I at least feel familiar, like a quirky acquaintance you talk about at social gatherings.
