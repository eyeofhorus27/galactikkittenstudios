  • QWN Website

    Web Design Copywrite Creative Direction

  • Client
    Quintessential Wonder Nasty

  • Role
    Web Designer & Copywriter

Quintessential Wonder Nasty is as bold and outlandish as its name suggest. It is an LA born LGBTQ Burlesque group with a mission to spread inclusiveness, radical self-expression and educate around what it means to be queer. For this very reason QWN demanded a site that would showcase this colorful explosion and "in your face" mentality, which represents these quirky individuals. In addition, I wanted the site to convey a sense of togetherness that coexists with the free expression of the indiosycratic weirdness that is unique to each character.

To convey togetherness, I went with a single-page application in which I create a stacking effect of images via parallaxes so that they all blend into each other. A bit like the ending scene of "The Rocky Horror Picture Show." To portray the eccentricity and uniqueness of each character, I created a section with their bios & photos.

On the other hand, I made the contact & services section look clean and easy to read for obvious reasons (so that people can easily find how to contact the group for bookings. I can't believe you just made me explain this.) also serving as a nice contrast to the previous mendling of photos. Finally, I placed the mission and reel on the top to catch users' attention and also to immediately present what QWN is all about. The final result? A feeling you're right there with the audience watching the show. An immersive experience in what it means to be wild and free.
