This project was part of a startup competition I joined, through an incubator called Demium. We all got assigned in teams of 3 and had a weekend to come up with a startup idea and how we were going to execute it.  Born from this was Creatix Hub, a platform where content creators would be able to monetize their talents by showcasing their work and receiving targeted traffic, unlike in platforms like Patreon.

When the execution phase began, I was responsible for all things design and marketing. I created an Instagram, Facebook, wrote a marketing strategy proposal, built a Brand Book Guide and designed our Pitch Deck. In the end my team won and our Pitch Deck was voted the best one. 

The colors I chose conveyed the idea of Hygge, which in Danish is the concept of feeling cozy and enjoying the little things in life. Content creators do what they love, yet often feel unsure about the financial aspect, so we wanted them to enjoy their process, while feeling at peace that we have the rest taken care of. For a logo I chose an origami crane, a symbol of meticulous skill, talent and elegance. In regards to our marketing strategy, I conducted a competitive analysis, sent out Facebook surveys to find out what our target audience's main pain points were, researched channels of advertising and promotion and built up our Instagram and Facebook engagement and traffic.
