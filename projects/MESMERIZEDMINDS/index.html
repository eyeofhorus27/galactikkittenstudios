  • Mesmerized Minds

    Web Design Copywrite

  • Client
    Julian Ulfano

  • Role
    Web Designer & Copywriter

This is one of the first websites I ever built for a client. Julian has a Hypnotherapy practice and he wanted me to build a site that he could send potential clients to in order to learn more about him and book a session. I wanted the website to exude a sense of trust, especially since people have a lot of misconceptions regarding Hypnosis. Moreover, I wanted his potential clients to feel at peace since relaxation is one of the main characteristics of Hypnosis. In addition, I wanted to imply a connection with our higher-selves and the divine via images of nature.

To accomplish this I used earthy tones and various imagery of serene yet mysterious environments (representing the subconscious), such as mountains surrounded by clouds and waters reflecting sunsets. Julian's target audience also happens to be a lot of people who care about self-development and yoga, hence why I also made the site feel like an upscale yoga retreat brochure.
